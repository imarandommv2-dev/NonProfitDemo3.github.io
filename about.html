Who We Are

A Home Away From Home

The Azerbaijani Cultural Center Of Washington State (ACC-WA) is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit organization serving the Azerbaijani community and those who cherish its rich heritage across the Pacific Northwest.

Founded by passionate members of the diaspora, ACC-WA serves as a gathering place for cultural education, language preservation, civic engagement, and the celebration of Azerbaijani traditions. We are dedicated to fostering cross-cultural understanding and building lasting bonds between our community and the broader Washington State public.

With a presence in both Seattle and Redmond, we are uniquely positioned to serve the tech-forward, diverse communities of the Eastside and the vibrant, cosmopolitan spirit of Seattle.

Our Mission

To preserve, promote, and celebrate Azerbaijani culture, language, and heritage in Washington State — fostering unity within the diaspora and enriching the Pacific Northwest's multicultural fabric.

◈

Our Vision

A thriving, well-connected Azerbaijani community in Washington — recognized for its cultural contributions, civic leadership, and the warm hospitality that defines Azerbaijani identity worldwide.
