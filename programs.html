Our Programs

What We Offer

Programs & Initiatives

◆

Our programs are designed to serve the Azerbaijani community at every stage of life — from young children discovering their roots to elders preserving their legacy.

Sports

Adult Soccer Gatherings

Friendly matches and soccer gatherings for adults. A wonderful way to stay active, build teamwork, and connect with other community members over a shared love of the sport.

  • Weekly matches in local Redmond parks
  • Open to all skill levels
  • Focus on wellness and community building
  • Post-game social gatherings
Join a Match →
Education

Kids Weekend School

Saturday cultural and educational classes for kids. We offer interactive learning, storytelling, basic language lessons, and fun creative crafts designed for young children.

  • Classes every Saturday morning
  • Ages 5–12 welcome
  • Azerbaijani history & folklore
  • Fun crafts, singing, and games
Register Child →
Culture

Azerbaijani Çay Circle

Traditional tea gatherings where community members connect, discuss heritage, share stories, and enjoy traditional sweets like pakhlava and shekerbura in Redmond.

  • Authentic Azerbaijani black tea
  • Traditional sweets and treats
  • Informal, warm cultural discussion
  • Everyone is welcome to join
Attend Circle →
Support

Newcomer Support

Helping newly arrived individuals and families settle into Redmond and the Eastside. We provide resources, guidance, networking, and support links.

  • Settle-in resources and checklists
  • Mentorship from established members
  • Local community connection links
  • Language and document translation help
Get Support →
Language

Language Classes

Formal Azerbaijani language classes for youth, heritage speakers, and partners. Structured curriculum focusing on conversation, grammar, reading, and writing.

  • Experienced native-speaking tutors
  • Beginner and intermediate cohorts
  • Curriculum tailored for kids & adults
  • Hybrid online and in-person options
Enroll Now →
Family

Multicultural & Blended Family Meetup

A welcoming space for blended families to connect, share experiences, and integrate Azerbaijani culture, language, and traditions into their daily lives.

  • Quarterly family meetups & picnics
  • Cultural exchange discussions
  • Support resources for blended couples
  • Fun environment for kids & parents
